Mushroom risotto recipe

Description

Preparation of a mushroom risotto, we will need ingridients and to follow some steps you can read about these to right below! (we assume you already have salt and pepper)

Ingridients

  • 1 envelope of Gourmet Dehydrated Mushrooms
  • 400 ml of water
  • 1 sachet of Gourmet Vegetable Powdered Broth
  • 2 tsp of olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced small
  • 1 tsp Gourmet Thyme Leaves
  • 400g of cleaned and sliced ​​mushrooms
  • 500g of rice
  • 200 ml of white wine
  • 1 tsp Gourmet Sea Salt, Garlic and Parsley
  • 1 tbsp salted butter
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Gourmet Ground Black Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, hydrate the dehydrated mushrooms for at least 30 minutes in 1/2 of cold water or white wine.
  2. Strain the broth and add it to a pot along with 400 ml of water. Add the Vegetable Broth Powder, stir, and cook over low heat.
  3. In a large pot, heat the olive oil, then add the onion and cook until soft and translucent. Add the chopped rehydrated mushrooms and thyme leaves and cook for one minute.
  4. Add the mushrooms and cook until soft.
  5. Add the rice and cook, stirring, over medium heat for approximately 5 minutes or until the rice turns opaque white.
  6. Add the white wine and cook until completely absorbed.
  7. Add the sea salt, garlic, and parsley, and gradually add the hot broth, one cup at a time. Wait until all the broth is absorbed before adding the next cup. Stirring is not necessary, but it is necessary to do so frequently.
  8. After 15 minutes, start tasting the rice, as it should be al dente. The total cooking time is 18 to 20 minutes.
  9. Once the rice is ready, turn off the heat and add the butter, grated Parmesan cheese, and ground black pepper. Stir to combine well and serve immediately.

    10. ENJOY!
