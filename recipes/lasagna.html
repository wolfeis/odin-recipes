Lasagna recipe

Description

Preparation of a tomato sauce lasagna, we will need ingridients and to follow some steps you can read about these to right below!

Ingridients

  • Basil
  • 500 gr of grounded beef
  • Olive oil
  • 1lt of a good tomato sauce
  • 250mg of slices of pre cooked lasagna dough
  • 300mg of parmesano cheese

Steps

  1. In a pan cook, for 25 min, and mix the basil, the 500mg of gorunded beef and 100ml of olive oil, add salt and pepper
  2. In a glass tray put some olive oil, spread properly
  3. In the same tray and when the sauce is ready,set slices of the pre cooked lasagna dough, then cheese and then sauce until full

ENJOY!
