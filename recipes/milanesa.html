Chicken milanesa recipe

Description

Preparation of a chicken milanesa, we will need ingridients and to follow some steps you can read about these to right below! (we assume you already have salt and pepper)

Ingridients

  • 500gr of boneless chicken breast
  • 250gr of oat
  • 250gr of breadcrumbs
  • 500ml of sunflower seed oil
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs and pute them in a bowl, stir still it gets all yellow
  2. To this mix add salt and pepper as you please
  3. In a plate put the oat and the breadcrumbs, mix them
  4. Cut the chicken breast in uniform slices
  5. Pass the chicken first for the egg mix and then the oat and breadcrumbs mix
  6. In a pan, put sunflower seed oil generously
  7. When the oil is hot enough start frying the chicken breasts until they're brown

ENJOY!
